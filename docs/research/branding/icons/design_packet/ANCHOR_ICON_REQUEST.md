### Visual rules (from our VISUAL_LANGUAGE.md)

- **"Glow, don't tint"** — Blue is light emission at edges, not color fill
- **"Light emerging from dark"** — The darkness is the canvas, blue is energy
- **ECG Blue:** #0099FF primary, #33b1ff bright variant, #66ccff highlight
- **Body material:** Dark glass (#0f1525 to #060a12 range)
- **Edge glow** is directional: brighter at bottom, dimmer at top
- **No traditional tab bump** on the folder — the tab flows naturally
- **Diagonal glass reflection** across the front face
- **The system is alive** — these icons should look like nodes in a living system
